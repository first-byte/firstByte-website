 From on prem, to public clouds, and blockchains - what’s the future of infrastructure?
All Levels
In the past two decades we’ve shifted our compute from on premise servers to shared public clouds. What is the next generation of infrastructure going to look like? In this talk, Cooper Kunz, a developer evangelist at Hedera Hashgraph and previous organizer of Hack Arizona, will dive into the technologies moving us beyond APIs and trusted 3rd parties to the next era of the web, web 3. This includes projects like Tim Berners-Lee’s Solid, the Ethereum Blockchain, the Interplanetary File System (IPFS), and more. Finally, we’ll dive into hands on examples with JavaScript, showing how easy it is to use and add to your project or resume.
